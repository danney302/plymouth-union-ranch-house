Availability & Rates

The home features 3 bedrooms and 2 lofts in a classic Vermont farmhouse — each area is suitable for a couple or two close friends.

Warning - Construction is ongoing for the 2025–26 Season

Flat Rate (Nov 1 – May 31) - 7 Months of shared use

  • Single occupancy: $3,750 per half-share
  • Double occupancy (two people sharing one sleeping area):$4,750 per half-share

A half-share includes:

  • Two set weekends per month (Fri–Sun nights)
  • Up to 10 midweek nights per month
  • Private room access, shared use of house amenities (kitchen, hot tub, etc.)
  • Guests welcome with prior approval: $65/night per guest
  • No full shares offered – only four total half-shares available guarantees plenty of room
  • Hot tub use and access - the hot tub is a best effort amenity, and will be available as conditions allow.
